About EXIF Data

EXIF (Exchangeable Image File Format) data is metadata stored in image files. It can include:

  • GPS coordinates (exact location where photo was taken)
  • Camera/phone model and manufacturer
  • Date and time the photo was taken
  • Camera settings (aperture, shutter speed, ISO)
  • Thumbnail images

All processing happens locally in your browser - your images are never uploaded to any server.
